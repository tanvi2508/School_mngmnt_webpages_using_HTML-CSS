Ms.Abby Domingo


Abby Domingo is looking forward to another incredible school year! She loves to inspire and encourage students here at Ashley Falls to be the best they can be in and out of the classroom! Mrs. Domingo began working for the Del Mar Union School District in 2003. She taught for 11 years in DMUSD and in the Boston Public Schools in Massachusetts. After being the Assistant Principal in our district, she joined Ashley Falls as the Principal in 2015 and there is no other place she would rather be!

Abby grew up in Massachusetts and received her Teaching Credential from the University of Massachusetts-Amherst, along with her Bachelors of Arts in Elementary Education and Sociology. Mrs. Domingo completed her Masters in Educational Leadership and earned her Administration Credential from Point Loma Nazarene University. She also holds a California Multiple Subject Teaching Credential and a Crosscultural, Language & Academic Development (CLAD) certificate.
